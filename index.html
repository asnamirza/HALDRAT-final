About HALDR

The logistic drone will be equipped with the following features:
• Payload capacity: Minimum 40 kg
• Altitude range: 9,000 ft - 16,000 ft
• Wind resistance: 50 km/h - 70 km/h
• Temperature tolerance: -35°C TO - 55°C
• Advanced navigation system: For precise flight and obstacle avoidance
• Long-range communication capabilities: For reliable data transmission and control
The drone will be designed to operate autonomously, with human intervention only required for mission planning and monitoring. It will be capable of landing and taking off from various terrains, including landing pads and open fields.
